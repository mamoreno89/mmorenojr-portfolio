Case Studies

Timekeeping Fraud Investigation

Challenge

Identify the root cause of timekeeping irregularities driving unexplained labor costs without disrupting operations.

Approach

Correlated HR, access control, and endpoint logs using Magnet AXIOM, X‑Ways, and Power BI; partnered with HR, Legal, and Compliance; preserved Chain of Custody.

Outcome

Confirmed coordinated falsification; recovered $250,000+ and delivered defensible reporting.

Translation

Established a proactive fraud‑detection workflow embedded in compliance reviews.

Vehicle Forensics SOP Implementation

Challenge

Stand up a standardized, defensible process for IVI/telematics acquisition and analysis.

Approach

Authored and validated SOPs mapped to SWGDE, ISO/IEC 17025, ANAB; leveraged Berla iVe, Magnet AXIOM, and Cellebrite UFED.

Outcome

Processed 25+ examinations in the first quarter; recovered GPS routes, paired‑device logs, and trip data; expanded capabilities by 35%; saved $60K/year.

Translation

Reusable framework for future service rollouts and ANAB audit readiness.

Cloud Activity Analytics

Challenge

Too many unstructured user activity logs across email, Box, and SharePoint slowed investigations.

Approach

Built a Proofpoint + Varonis with Power Query/Excel pivots for behavioral flags and file movement visibility.

Outcome

Reduced review time by 70%; improved triage accuracy by 40%; faster exfiltration detection.

Translation

Repeatable template for insider‑risk and exfiltration analysis at scale.
